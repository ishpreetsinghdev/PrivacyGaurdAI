Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PrivacyGuard AI.

Is my data really private?

Absolutely! PrivacyGuard AI runs 100% locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server. We don't collect, store, or process your data anywhere. It's the most private way to scan for sensitive information.

How does it work without an internet connection?

The entire scanning engine runs in JavaScript directly in your browser. Once the page loads, you can even go offline and it will continue to work. All pattern matching happens locally on your device.

What types of data can it detect?

We detect 55+ risk patterns including: SSN, credit cards, phone numbers, emails, IP addresses, API keys, passwords, scam indicators, phishing tactics, corporate confidential markers, and more. The detection engine is constantly improving.

Is it free to use?

Yes! The core scanning features are free forever with no limits. We're currently in beta, so all features including advanced analytics and history are free. Paid plans will add team features and premium support in the future.

Can I use it for work/business?

Absolutely! Many professionals use PrivacyGuard AI to check their AI prompts, emails, and documents before sharing. Since everything stays local, it's perfect for handling confidential business information. Business plans with team features are coming soon.
